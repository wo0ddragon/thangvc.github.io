The High Priest’s Garments

Summary

  1. Their official garments were prepared [Exo 28]
  2. The garments made [Exo 28:4,40,42]
    1. High Priest Only
      1. A Breastpiece,
      2. An Ephod,
      3. A robe,
      4. A Turban
      5. A Woven Tunic,
      6. A embroidered Sash
    2. All Priests
      1. Tunics (woven like the high priest's?)
      2. Sashes (embroidered like the high priest's?)
      3. Head bands
      4. Linen undergarments
    3. The Materials used [Exo 28:5; 25:7]
      1. Gold and
      2. Blue,
      3. Purple and
      4. Scarlet yarn, and
      5. Fine linen

A TURBAN [Exo 28:36-41]

  1. Who for:
    1. High priest only
  2. Occasion/Use:
    1. “Continually” [Exo 28:38]
  3. Parts
    1. Turban [Exo 28:39-41
    2. Material: made of fine linen
    3. GOLD PLATE [Exo 28:36-38]
      1. Material:
        1. Pure gold
        2. Blue material for the cord that fastens the gold plate to the turban
      2. Engraving: “HOLY TO THE LORD” is to be engraved on it as on a seal
      3. Blue cord to fasten the gold plate to the turban [Exo 28:37]
      4. Purpose of Gold plate:
        1. So that the sacred gifts that the Israelites consecrate to Yahweh may be acceptable to Him
        2. Aaron bears the guilt involved in the sacred gifts of the Israelites. That is, Aaron represents all the gifts, Yahweh accepts Aaron in place of all the gifts. Thus, if Aaron is “Holy to Yahweh”, so are the sacred gifts of the Israelites. Another way of stating it is that God deals with Israel through Aaron—Aaron represents Israel to God (with regards to the sacred things).

A ROBE [Exo 28:31-35]

  1. Who for:
    1. High priest only
  2. Occasion/Use:
    1. Whenever he ministers [Exo 28:35]
  3. Material
    1. Entirely of blue cloth [Exo 28:31]
    2. Blue, purple and scarlet yarn for the pomegranates around the edge of the garment [Exo 28:33]
    3. Gold for the gold bells between the pomegranates [Exo 28:33]
  4. Parts
    1. An opening for the head in the center [Exo 28:32]
    2. A woven edge like a collar around the opening [Exo 28:32]. This is so that it will not tear
    3. The edge of the garment. Around the edge of the garment are to be
      1. Pomegranates of blue, purple and scarlet yarn [Exo 28:33]. And
      2. Golden bells [Exo 28:33-34]. The bells are to alternate with the pomegranates
        1. Purpose of bells: to announce the entry of the high priest into the Holy Place so that he does not die [Exo 28:35]
    4. A Woven TUNIC [Exo 28:39-41]
